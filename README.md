# Credit-Card-Default-rate contribution analysis-PowerBI
Analysis of Key Drivers's contribution in  default rate using Power BI, identifying high-risk customers based  and credit limit  behavior, financial Factors etc.

***

📊 Project Overview
---
This project showcases a dynamic Power BI dashboard built that shows Default Risk Contribution or Key Drivers Across customer's Demographhics like Age group, Educational Level, Gemder, Credit limit, Age & Credit limit
and Default Rate over time , Overall distribution with Tree visual 

***
🎯 Objectives
---
 - Analyze Which Age group Contributee the  Most
 - Identify which Gender Contributed the most
 - Drill down Gender Vs Age for Deep Analysis 
 - Indentify the Credit Limit Effect on Defaulting (Low,Medium, high)
 - Analyze the defautl Rate Trend over time 
 - Identified the Top Most Contributer in Default Rate
 - Drill down Gender vs Credit limit to see the utilization%
 - Identify Educational Effect on Defaulting
***

📌 Key Insights
---
- (22%)+ Customers were default Customers
- Females were the highest contributer with (12.54%) which is (4%) more then the Mens(9.58%)
- (20-30) and (31-40) **Age group** were the most contributer with the values (8.24%) and (7.30%) combined (15.54%) Applies for both Male and Female
- Customers with **Low Credit Limit ** Defaulted the most with the value (17.40%) which which is (78%) of total Defaul Rate (22.12%)
- As the month increses No of Payment delays increase on an Average by (1k+)
- Customers with utilization % 1.5 times Definatly Defaults
- Customer with university and Graduate Educational Background defaults most with (11.10%) and (6.79%) combined (17.89%)
- Total Customrs Customr (30K)
- Average Utilization % /cust is (223.83%)
- (₹7.15bn)  Amount is at Risk
-  Amount Recoverd (₹949.54M)
- Revenue Generated (454.23 M) :- KPI insights
